ABOUT

The Kings of the Underground Recording Studios was founded in 2000 by Wyclef Gin of The Fugees and Jerry 'Wonda' Duplessis, a multi-platinum Grammy-winning producer. The two came along with the goal of giving artists an unparalleled studio experience. By focusing on sound quality, performance and privacy, they created a world-class space where artists instantly feel at home, can enter their creative flow and record the next big hit.
